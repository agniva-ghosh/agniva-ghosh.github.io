About Me

I'm Agniva (Bengali: অগ্নিভ, pronounced as 'ogni-vo') and welcome to my personal webpage.

Currently I'm a Physics Graduate student at the University of Minnesota in the United States with extensive experience in programming, statistics and mathematical modeling. I've done my bachelors and masters from Serampore College (University of Calcutta) and Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur in India.

At University of Minnesota, I am working with Professor Liliya Williams. My current research is primarily focussed on Strong Gravitational Lensing. Most of my research projects primarily focussed on finding free-form non-parametric inversions of galaxy clusters using strong gravitational lensing, dark matter dynamics in galaxy clusters and predictions of precision measurement of the Hubble constant using reconstructed time delay estimations.
